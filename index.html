Uttarakhand Decision Support System for Disaster Risk Reduction

A geospatial platform for effective and efficient decisions

Find Out More

Following the cloud outburst in June 2013, the World Bank has financed the Uttarakhand Disaster Recovery Project (UDRP). One of the subcomponents of the URDP focuses on the establishment of systems for disaster risk management and risk-informed development planning, with a specific focus on enhancing hazard monitoring and early warning, preparedness, response, and recovery planning. The goal of this project was to implement a collaborative geospatial platform; integrate real-time and baseline data into the platform; deploy applications on the platform to support the emergency operation centres (EOC) and decision-makers involved in disaster management in the state of Uttarakhand.

About US

Uttarakhand State Disaster Management Authority (USDMA) is a part of State Government and is a nodal agency for planning, coordination and monitoring activities related to disaster prevention, mitigation, preparedness and management. USDMA lays down policies on disaster management for the state. It approves disaster management plan in accordance with the guidelines laid down by the National Authority and coordinates its implementation. It provides guidelines and reviews the measures being taken for mitigation, capacity building and preparedness by the Government and issue guidelines as necessary. USDMA recommends the provision of funds for mitigation and preparedness measures. Uttarakhand State Disaster Management Authority (USDMA) was set up and notified as per sub-section (1) of section 14 of the Disaster Management Act 2005. The Uttarakhand Disaster Management Authority is chaired by honourable Chief Minister of the State.

A geospatial platform that integrates data from static and real-time data from different sources and having easy ETL for data integration is developed by the Asian Institute of Technology in association with Previstar. This system has an advance alert mechanism and a robust BCP for high availability and low fault. In additional functionality, Decision Support System supports access in any language; allows user to perform Geospatial as well as Statistical Analysis on data and have inbuilt routing and messing functionality. This System will facilitate monitoring and resource management for the Incident Response System and will be mainly managed by the Disaster Management Authority. Moreover, during a disaster, each government organization will have unique roles and responsibilities and we have incorporated all these by developing organization-specific user interfaces in the system. Thus, the system recognizes each organization through their log-in credentials and display only the organization-specific menus.

System Functionalities

Monitoring and Observations

Monitoring/Observation available information etc. provided within the agencies can be visualized on a map or a list.

Lifesaving

Decision Maker can visulaize static and near-real time lifesaving facilities available in proximity to indicdent and accordingly make effective decision.

Settlement of Emergency Operating Center

Effective and Efficient decisions can be given based on the information provided within system with the activation of Emergency Operating Center.

Incident reporting and Announcement

System has inbuilt mechanism for flow of information among users. Reporting of the ground situation can be synced into the system directly.

Monitor Shelter Establishment

Locations of Dedicated Shelters are mapped in the system along with the facility to request authority for addition shelter and management of shelter facilities.

Routing

This system has its open source Routing and Isochrone functionality within the system that helps user in identifying the best possible route.

System Highlights

Simple workflow of the system and easy design to make sure that even staff not familiar with disaster response can learn it very quickly;

It is possible to cooperate with each other's correspondence and related disaster information in related prefectures, municipalities, municipalities and other organizations by API;

Screens, menus, etc. can be set by the user, and it is possible to support the system configuration consistent with the actual circumstances of each EOCs and Disasters Management Authorities. User management, task management, menu management, etc. can be done.

Alert Layers

Alert Layers can be visualized as OGC WMS Services.
Modelled Hazard Maps

Modelled hazard maps for flood and landslide are integerated in the system.
Inbuilt Messaging Functionality

System is capable of sending mails to user using the system and to individual from within the system.
Near-Real Time Ambulance status

108 Ambulance service provides information in Uttarakhand about their ambulance current status.
Open Route Services

System has integrated OpenRouteService for Routing and Isochrone.
Incident Response System

This part of the system manages the resources available in the region.
